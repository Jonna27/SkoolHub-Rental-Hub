ABOUT


The Student Council of Engineering and Architecture serves as the umbrella organization of the Association of Geodetic Engineering Students (AGES), Institute of Computer Engineers of the Philippines - Student Edition (ICpEP.SE), Institute of Integrated Electrical Engineers - Council of Student Chapters (IIEE-CSC), Junior Institute of Electronics Engineers of the Philippines (JIEcEP), Junior Philippine Institute of Civil Engineers (JPICE), Junior Philippine Society of Mechanical Engineers (JPSME), and United Architects of the Philippines Student Auxiliary (UAPSA).
