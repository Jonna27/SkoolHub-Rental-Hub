ABOUT MISSION
As an organization, UCS shall hone the skills and abilities of every scholar through immersion of community engagement and initiation of formation programs that could lead to nation building.

VISION By 2022, the UCS shall be an organization for city scholars in USTP that does not only manifest academic excellence but also embodies the sense of active social involvement towards the betterment of Cagayan de Oro City
